The Online Research Database Service (ORDS) enables you to upload or create research databases which can then be edited and shared online. You can open up your data, or subsets of it, to fellow researchers or the even the general public, maximising the impact of the information behind your publications.

Using the ORDS for your data brings many benefits. Databases in the ORDS are backed up every night, can be accessed across the Internet and can be integrated into appropriate data archives for long term preservation. Data and query results can be exported into almost all popular data manipulation and visualization software, including Microsoft Excel.
